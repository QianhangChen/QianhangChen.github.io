About Me

I'm an MSc student in Astronomy & Astrophysics at the University of Amsterdam. My research interests include ultracompact X-ray binaries, disk winds, jets, and X-ray spectroscopy with XRISM.

Current Projects

  • XRISM spectroscopy of 4U 1916-053
  • Bow-shock survey of X-ray binaries (WISE + RACS)
  • Spectroscopic campaign on the colliding-wind binary WR 140

XRISM Spectroscopy — 4U 1916-053

High-resolution line diagnostics to probe disk winds and ionized absorbers in an ultracompact X-ray binary.

Bow-Shock Survey of X-ray Binaries

Cross-matching WISE infrared and RACS radio data to identify bow shocks and study stellar feedback into the ISM.

WR 140 Spectroscopic Campaign

Multi-night observations with HERMES/Mercator to study wind acceleration, clump dynamics, and LDI signatures.
